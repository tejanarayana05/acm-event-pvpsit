CODETITAN 2K23

The ACM student chapter of Prasad V. Potluri Siddhartha Institute of Technology, Vijayawada are excited to announce the phenomenal event of the year - CODETITAN 2K23, a national wide coding contest conducted on 19th October, 2023. All the techsavyys out there join the quest and compete with other like-minded individuals to win the prestigious title of the CODE TITAN and exciting cash prizes. Whether you are novice coder or a coding master this is your oppurtunity to shine. Register Today!!

Event Details

Bits & Bytes

Overall Impression: Are you eligible to be a titan? Every participant will get a set of questions to be answered within 30 minutes. Brush up your Computer Science Fundamentals and aptitude and get ready to tackle questions based on Technology Awareness + Techstack & Computer Science Fundamentals.

Code & Cascade

Overall Impression:All round 1 qualified students are eligible to take part in this round.In this round, participants will put their coding skills to test with our carefully curated coding challenges. Whether you are a rookie coder or a seasoned veteran, this is your call to write a cascading code with optimised time and space complexity.

PS: If the participant is found to have plagiarised or engaged in unethical behaviour, their quest ends here. (Unqualified for additional rounds).

Code Converse

Overall Impression:In this round, We would like converse 1:1, with all the titans who made it thus far. We would want to evaluate your knowledge of computer science, While we dive deep about your experiences/works and other aspects related to the field.

Code of Conduct

  • Participants are expected to compete fairly and honestly, without using any form of cheating or plagiarism.
  • All code submitted must be the participant's original work and submitted within the designated time.
  • Ensure that your code adheres to the contest's rules regarding language usage, libraries, and external resources.
  • Unauthorized collaboration or sharing of code during the contest may result in disqualification.
  • Contest organizers may impose penalties for code of conduct violations, including warnings, disqualification, and bans from future events.
